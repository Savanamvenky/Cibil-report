Welcome

CIBIL Score is a 3 digit numeric summary of your credit history, derived by using details found in the 'Accounts' and 'Enquiries' sections on your CIBIL Report and ranges from 300 to 900. The closer your score is to 900, the higher are the chances of your loan application getting approved.

Payment History

Making late payments or defaulting your EMI's or dues will negetively impact your score.

Hight Credit Utilization

An increase in the current balance of your credit cart indicates an increased repayment burden and may negetively affect your sc.

Credit Mix

Having a balanced mix of your secured and unsecured loans is likely to have more a more positive effect on your score.
